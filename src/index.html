What are the different type of rock climbing?

Rock climbing consists of three types; bouldering, top rope and lead climbing.

Bouldering

Bouldering is where no ropes or harness is needed. The only equipment need for bouldering is a pair of climbing shoes and lots of chalk.

Top rope

Top rope requires the climbers to have a level 1 belay certification. For top rope, the anchor is at the highest point of the wall. The belayer will then belay the climber with a belaying device while the climber climbs.

Lead Climbing

This type of climbing are suitable for individuals with climbing experience. It requires level 2 belay certification The climber will anchor himself while he climbs.
